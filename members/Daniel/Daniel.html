Why did I come to VEX V5?

I came to VEX V5 to learn about the structures of robots, to learn the different ways people use robots, how coding controls the robots, what the robots could do, to be risk taking, to try things that I’ve never done before (VEX). I also need to work on my bravery, my confidence, I cannot stand in front of people without getting nervous, I will be practicing this and getting better by controlling the robot in front of many people, and the camera, which is a type of competing in the VEX V5.

What role did I play in PLR?

The role I am playing in PLR is the sub-driver, the person that drives the robot if the main driver couldn’t drive, or something happens. I practice with our captain, the main driver, training the same thing, but my score is lower.
